## 2. Description of Service

Deka English is a vocabulary learning application that helps you study English using spaced repetition (SRS). The App provides:

- **Flashcard management** — create, edit, organize, and review vocabulary cards
- **Spaced repetition scheduling** — automatic review scheduling based on your performance
- **Deck and tag organization** — group cards into decks and label them with tags
- **AI-assisted card drafting** (optional) — generate card content using an AI proxy server connected to OpenAI's API
- **AI-assisted practice** (optional) — practice vocabulary through AI-generated prompts with answer checking
- **Backup and restore** — export and import your cards as JSON files

The App operates on a **local-first** architecture. Your data is stored on your device and is not uploaded to any server unless you explicitly use the AI-assisted features.
